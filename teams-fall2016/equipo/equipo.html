Welcome to equipo-dev!

Equipo is a tool to help distribute experience among project groups. Currently, it is in development for use in future CS480 courses, but we eventually hope to let any professor in Cal Poly Pomona use it to help create diverse groups.

Why Equipo?

During our time as students, we have noticed that when we are assigned a group project one of three things happen:

1. Friends stick with friends
2. People with the similar knoweledge group up
3. People get into groups without knowing the skill sets of the other people in the group.

By generating groups based on skill levels and personalities, we will create an ideal group environment where everyone can learn a lot.
