Introducing bookXchange

♦♦♦

Every quarter/semester, all students face the issue of buying new textbooks for all their class and selling their already used textbooks. However, all the books are either expensive or difficult to find. Students have tried Amazon, Ebay, or Facebook. bookXchange is a centralized place where students from each campus can go to buy or sell their textbooks to other students at their own school.
