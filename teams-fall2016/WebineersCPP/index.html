About

ClassTrack is a website designed to help students keep track of the courses they have taken and the ones that they still need to take. Once you have taken a class you can simply highlight that course in any color, and you will know that you have already taken that course, that way when next semester rolls around, you know what classes you still need to take in order to graduate!.
