Problem Overview

Have you ever driven around the country and wonder whether the area that you are currently in safe?

Have you ever got lost and wonder whether this is safe?

Would a service which can tell you the answer straight away without reading all the statistics be wonderful?

The solution is here!

"Am I Safe Here?" web service is coming up!!!

Features!!!

  1. Get your current location via HTML5 Geolocation and uses crime statistics to determine the city you are in is safe!!

  2. Improve the the city feature & Focus on the Neighborhood

  3. Globalize the feature to other countries...

  4. Navigation System with safest routes
